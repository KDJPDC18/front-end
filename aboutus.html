Our Business

Our Story

Kayle Brews began its journey in May 2024, brewed from the passion of a young coffee enthusiast, Kyle Suarez. With a deep love for coffee and a dream to create a cozy space for everyone to enjoy, Kyle brought his favorite brewing method—Cold Brew—to the forefront of his menu.

Our Mission

At Kayle Brews, our mission is to serve comfort, connection, and a little bit of Kyle’s story in every sip. We believe in quality, community, and creating a welcoming space for all coffee lovers.

Our Values

Quality

We never compromise on the quality of our coffee, ingredients, or our drinks. Every cup is crafted with care and passion.

Innovation

We love to experiment with new flavors and brewing techniques to bring you unique and exciting drinks and snacks.

Community

We’re proud to be part of our local community and strive to give back through events and partnerships.

Sustainability

We’re committed to reducing our environmental impact by using sustainable packaging and sourcing local ingredients whenever possible.

Our Products

Product Ingredients

We believe that the best drinks and snacks start with the best ingredients. That's why we source high-quality, fresh beans, milk, and flavorings from trusted suppliers. Whenever possible, we use locally sourced, organic ingredients to ensure maximum freshness and flavor.

Our key ingredients include:

  • Sagada arabica coffee beans
  • Premium matcha
  • Fresh milk and cream
  • Good quality of fruit purees and syrups
  • Locally sourced snacks and add-ons

Preparation Process

Each of our cold brew drinks and snacks is prepared with care to ensure quality and consistency. At Kayle Brews, our baristas use time-tested cold brew methods combined with modern techniques to achieve the perfect flavor and smoothness in every cup. Meanwhile, our kitchen staff freshly fries every batch of snacks to golden perfection.

Our preparation process includes:

  • Slow-steeped cold brew crafted in small batches
  • Freshly fried snacks made to order
  • Strict quality control checks before serving
  • Thoughtful packaging to preserve freshness and presentation

Shipping & Delivery

We take great care to ensure that our products reach you in perfect condition. Our delivery and pick-up methods are designed to maintain the quality, freshness, and appearance of your orders from our shop to your hands.

Shipping details:

  • Local delivery available within Valenzuela City
  • Eco-friendly packaging materials
  • Express delivery options available
  • Contactless pick-up available at our shop

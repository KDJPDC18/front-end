Questions about Products

Are your drinks and snacks made fresh daily?
Yes, all our drinks and snacks are freshly prepared each day to ensure the best quality and taste for our customers.
Do you offer non-dairy or vegan options?
Yes, we offer non-dairy milk alternatives and some vegan snacks. Please ask our staff or check our menu for available options.
Can I customize my drink or snack order?
Absolutely! You can choose your preferred size, flavor, and add-ons for most drinks and snacks. Let us know your preferences when ordering.
How long do your products stay fresh?
Our drinks are best enjoyed immediately. Snacks are best consumed within the day for maximum freshness.
Are your ingredients locally sourced?
We strive to use locally sourced and high-quality ingredients whenever possible to support our community and ensure freshness.

Questions about Services

Do you offer delivery services?
Yes, we offer delivery within Valenzuela City. Delivery fees may apply depending on your location.
Can I order for pick-up?
Yes, you can place your order online and choose the pick-up option. We'll notify you when your order is ready.
Do you cater for events or parties?
We offer catering for small events and gatherings. Please contact us in advance to discuss your requirements.
How can I track my order?
After placing your order, you will receive updates via SMS or email. For further assistance, contact our customer support.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, GCash, and other major e-wallets. For online orders, payment instructions will be provided at checkout.

Miscellaneous Questions

What are your shop hours?
We are open daily from 5:00 PM to 10:00 PM. Hours may vary during holidays or special events.
Do you have a loyalty or rewards program?
Yes! Ask our staff about our loyalty program and how you can earn rewards with every purchase.
Are there discounts for bulk or group orders?
We offer discounts for bulk and group orders. Please contact us for more details and pricing.
Do you ship outside Valenzuela City?
Currently, we only deliver within Valenzuela City. For special requests, please contact us directly.
How can I contact customer support?
You can reach us via email at kaylebrews@gmail.com or call/text +63 956 2648 664. You may also use the inquiry form below.
